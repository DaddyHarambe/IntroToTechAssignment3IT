Detailed Description

Aim

The aim of our project is to develop an app that is friendly and adaptable for users. We envisioned that “beauty in minimal” this is because we wanted to create an app where people can view many brands and their clothes. We want to give the users all the best experience with FashStation. The group believes that teamwork is a required part of a project, as there wouldn’t be anything done and with the lack of communication, there would not be a product for the users.

Group Processes and communication

The group has all agreed to communicate our calls through Microsoft teams. Microsoft teams is an application that allows users to create groups and share their work. This allows easy access to view all our work together as we are using Microsoft teams word to write our project while having a meeting. Microsoft enables our teacher to join the meetings and view our documents when needed. The application lets us record the meetings which is needed in the assignment and if any of the group members forget their task, they can rewatch the meeting. The group agrees when we should have the next meeting after giving a set task during the call. Depending on how much work is given, the next meeting is at least every 3-4 to allows everyone to have time to get their set task done, unless we are very behind, we would have a meeting roughly 2-3. Communicating through the meetings reassures each member, letting them know what each person has completed and will be doing. If we need assistances from each other or letting each know information, we would communicate the messages through Facebook messenger. If in a case where we do not have communication with a group member where they do not reply, the group will contact the teacher letting them know that they have not been responding and if they have not done any work or their set task. If the person who has not been responding has not done any work, the rest of the members would evaluate and decide whether to wait for them to respond or continue the project without them. If we are behind due to a member not contributing, we would notify the teacher and possibly ask for special consideration.

Risks

There are many risks in doing this project, a risk that could be identified is that as the group is creating a new app, there is a possibility that we do not have enough knowledge to be able to produce a successful app. With this project having a limited timeframe, there would be possibility of the group no being able to learn the software and coding to create FashStation. As the project is based on an online shop, this concept is new to the members and require a lot of studying and learning. The software that is selected to create the app could be a software studying and trial and error as it is new to the members. Having many trials and error could also affect the chance of finish the project as there could be a problem is could not be fix due to our limit knowledge on the software. Furthermore, the risk of miss communication or the lack of communication before each member can affect the result of the project. This could happen due to a team member not cooperating and not implementing their work. Not implementing their work that was assigned today can delay the project as the work could be important and is needed before the project can continue. This could result in not being able to get as much that is done wanted or even complete the project in the set timeframe.

Another risk could be an operation risk, this is the change in the team processes. The group members could change roles unexpectedly could impact the project as the change in management or process could lead the team to have distractions and adjustments. These adjustments could take a while which may delay the project timeline. Moreover, the cost of the project could be a risk. This occurs when the spending of the budget is too high for the group’s budget. A high budget risk could be the software cost more than anticipated.
