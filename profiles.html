Career plans

At the stem, our jobs are relatively similar as it requires our technical thinking of technology to communicate back to the company or business as of which we are employed by. The role of a business analyst is to review how a business operates creating greater efficiencies for the future of the business while also leading project teams and relaying their knowledge and technical understanding back to the business. Whereas a Development Engineer (DevOps) an occupation which entails the engineer to merge and collaborate software development and IT operations to create more efficient and faster growth in the company’s software. However, business analyst (BA) differ from a DevOps engineer as a business analyst requires one to review current technologies within a business and implement existing software or processes that may increase its efficiency. This requires a (BA) to consistently communicate with the company and their team to lead them efficiently and effectively. DevOps, however, are typically more independent in their work and it is their role to create new software from scratch. A cybersecurity analyst is like DevOps as they typically work independently and require greater levels of technical thinking than a BA. Their role is to ensure a business is protected from cyber-attacks and does not require them to increase the efficiency of the business like a DevOps engineer or a BA.
