Skills and Jobs

We are a group consisting of three members with an ambitious dream and goal of making a successful fashion app for users of all ages to access. The app includes a feature for the user to input what types of clothing pieces they have in which the algorithm will generate an outfit befitting of what they own and have. However, this is not the only extent of the app as it also offers the user generated outfits with pieces of clothing of which the user does not yet own. The user may also wish to purchase the pieces of clothing directly from the app as the app will suggest the cheapest store for said clothes. This will also be done as at the beginning of the app the user will create a quick model of a person intended for the user to create a model of themselves or a model of another person to best see their outfits come to reality. However, our group members lack the skills, knowledge, time and funding to produce an application of such calibre. This is where we wish to have further assistance from those who are more experienced and specialise in their field. These roles include an app designer, backend developer, mobile developer and tech project manager. These 4 roles will provide the foundation and core for the development team as each role offers a crucial aspect to our application. The designer will provide the main aesthetics of the app. The backend developer will deal with the data used and imputed into the app while also maintaining the app. The mobile developer will have experience from generating and creating apps in past projects. Leading the whole team will be the tech project manager ensuring that all deadlines will be met and ensure social cohesion within the group. They will also manage where resources such as funding will be allocated to and determine what will need to be prioritised.

Therefore, this means that the designer must have good communication skills, team building skills and ideally an eye for aesthetics and aspects. These components should result in a successful app as the app would be pleasing to look at and easy to operate. Backend developers are crucial as they are responsible for managing data transactions between a user and the servers. The backend developer must be comfortable and confident with multiple programming languages, algorithms and data structuring. As it is their role to design and create the technology which will be the foundation for the app. Nevertheless, all successful apps require a solid foundation.
