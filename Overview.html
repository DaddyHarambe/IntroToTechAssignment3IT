Overview

Topic

The general outcome of this project is expected to be that we as a group develop an app that is both viable and fantastic to look at. We seek to build a fashion app that will change how people use and make fashion apps. We wish to provide not only well-crafted outfits for the user but also a way to provide them with the best deals for clothes. This should enable those who are previously hesitant to purchase clothes to finally get the piece they have always wanted or a way to reassure them that it may not be the best fit. However, the app is utilised we wish all those who operate and use the app benefit from it whether it be through a practical sense or a money saving one.

We hope not only to create a marvellous app but to also create a product that may be seen by larger companies to either further invest into our idea or for them to gain inspiration to create a greater app with a larger budget and more qualified team. Our only wish is to initialize something in a different app realm that has not yet been vastly explored yet which may see benefits if further invested into. This app has vast potential to grow with a more developed team. Furthermore, we wish to see where this app may take us with our current skill set. We potentially hope to allow the user to to further their creative mind with our app.

Motivation

Breaking into the IT industry as newcomers we as a group felt like we had to prove ourselves. We had to make an idea that was obtainable, realistic, and different. As a group we felt a pressure to impress especially to a wider audience. We find significance in this project as we believe all those who use it may benefit through its usage. There are few apps out there that do what we set this app out to do and we plan to change that. We plan to create a high-quality app that will fit in with modern trends and a user interface that will be up to notch with our competitors. Despite currently lacking the skills to create a full-fledged app we believe that our app FashStation may make a change within the fasion app realm. This app may spark a larger company to develop a greater app or provides us an opportunity to begin this revolution. Future employers may gravitate to this app through its aesthetically pleasing design, simple yet beautiful. This app will not only delineate our artistic design but also our ingenuity and technical knowledge. If successful we may program an algorithm that will generate vast amounts of clothing outfits many may have never thought of. Although a simple app we see much potential and hope that this is only the beginning of something larger.
